About Me

Experiences

I started my professional career at the beginning of 2018 as a water resources engineering staff for PT. Jasapatria Gunatama Engineering Consultant in Bandung City, Indonesia. I engaged in an irrigation network design project in Aceh Province. I performed the irrigation design, including hydraulic analysis and modelling for various hydraulic structures such as canals, aqueducts, culverts, off-takes, and drop structures. Surveying the existing irrigation structures and conducting outreach to stakeholders and local communities in the North Aceh Regency was a fresh and memorable experience.

After completing that project in 2019, I participated in a technical assistance project (TA 9634-REG) for the Asian Development Bank. As part of the project consultant with Landell Mills Limited, I was responsible for developing a national GIS roadmap to integrate flood risk management in Indonesia as a Junior GIS and Database Specialist. Over 8000 flood risk management projects have been compiled into a comprehensive GIS database. I also actively assisted in a capacity-building activity by preparing maps and training materials and sharing the GIS roadmap development in training sessions to reinforce stakeholders from the government staff in decision-making on flood risk project investments. The project was accomplished in 2022. This project was presented at the EGU General Assembly 2022 and the full abstract was published on its website.

Currently, I am working as a freelance GIS Specialist and contributing to society through water and mapping projects. Feel free to reach out for any collaboration and project opportunities.

Education

I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in water resources engineering and management from Institut Teknologi Bandung, Indonesia.
